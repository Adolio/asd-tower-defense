ASD - Tower Defense - About

This game has been the subject of two projects within an engineering school in Switzerland. The first part (v1.0) was conducted during a course of Algorithms and Data Structures (where ASD from French). This latter was then taken (v2.0) for a course in Software Engineering (GEN) to integrate a network game mode. It has also been improved on a slew of other aspects including the game engine, the GUI , design, etc.. If this project interests you, you can follow its progress through our website : http://www.asd-td.com/
