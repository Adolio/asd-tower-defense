ASD - Tower Defense - Field editor.

This page present you how to create your own map for ASD - Tower Defense.

What's a map ?
A map is a file which contains all information about a game session.

That is to say : the field with their options and all teams with their area (castle and creatures starts). For now, the waves of creatures are not yet configurable...

The field editor allow you to create this file.
What contains the field?
The field contains a lot of parameters about the game session in general.

In options of the terrain , for example, you can choice how many coins and number of lifes the player will have in the begining of the party.

The field also contains walls. Walls can't be destroyed by the player and walls force all creatures to skip next to them.
