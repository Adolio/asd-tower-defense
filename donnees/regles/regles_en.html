ASD - Tower Defense - Rules of the game.

The aim of this game is to strategically build a defense using towers to prevent waves of creatures to reach a critical area.

Creatures
Role
The creatures move in waves to the starting area [START] and try to reach your critical zone [FINISH]. If a creature reaches your critical zone, you lose a life [].

Types
Creatures can be of two types: land or air. The airlines are flying above the towers while the earth shall overcome them.

Gain
The death of a creature you to earn gold coins []. You can use these coins to buy towers.

Launch Wave
You decide when to initiate waves of creatures by clicking the appropriate button.
Towers
Role
The towers are there to prevent the creatures from reaching your critical zone. They take with regular rhythm on the creatures to destroy them.

Blocking
When you put a tower on the ground, the tower will block only the path of earthly creatures. These must then move around the tower. You will be able to force the path of earthly creatures.

Types
There are different types of towers with its own characteristics as the Tower of ice to slow the creatures. Some towers attack the earth and other creatures that air.

Purchase
The tours cost a certain amount of gold coins. It is sometimes wiser to wait to save for access to more powerful towers.

Upgrade / Sales
UA tower may also be improved or sold at any time. The improvements will cost you gold coins. In a sale, you'll earn 60% of the total price (with upgrades).
Field
Role
It provides a space for building towers, there is also the starting area and the critical area.

Path requires
The land does not pose a tower if the tower blocks the path of earthly creatures making it impossible to access the critical zone.

Walls
Parts of the land are not buildable, you will not be able to place towers.
Stars
You earn stars by the value of your score []. The stars will give you access to new playgrounds.
